About

GameDB is a website built by you. You add the information about your favourite games and your own reviews. Your contributions to the website will help others learn about your favourite games. Simply Sign up or Login to begin.

How to use
  1. Firstly Create an Account, or if you have done already Login.
  2. Click on Add Game.
  3. Enter the details about your chosen game, all fields are required.
  4. Press submit and view your game on the Games Page.
  5. You can edit a game at anytime by clicking the edit button its game page.*
  6. You can also delete games by pressing the delete button on its game page.*
*Only possible when the review belongs to you.
